About Us

At Detailing by Daniel, we focus on high quality service every time—while also making sure customers feel heard, respected, and supported from start to finish.

Whether you’re booking your first appointment or you’re a returning customer, you can expect reliable work, clear communication, and results you’ll be proud of.

Service

Service includes:

  • Carpet cleaning
  • Pet hair removal
  • Window cleaning
  • Seat cleaning
  • Leather cleaning
  • Basic through detailing that still achieves the look of a deep cleaning job
  • Use chemicals to help clean out the interior of car

What Service doesn’t include:

  • Steam cleaning (Not using a steamer)
  • Deep cleaning
  • The use of a carpet extractor
  • No sports car cleaning
  • Exterior cleaning
  • Suede cleaning

Pricing Range

Interior detailing prices range from $80.00 to $130.00.

The exact price depends on the size of the car and how dirty it is when the job is performed. In general, the larger the vehicle and the dirtier the interior, the higher the price will be within that range.

Note: You don’t pay when you schedule your appointment. Payment is due when the service is completed on your scheduled day.
